About Me

Hi there! I'm Erin Zielke, and I'm passionate about technology, problem-solving, and creative design. With a background in Computer Information Systems and a focus on tech support, I thrive in environments where I can blend my technical skills with my love for learning and helping others.

I've always embraced new challenges and aim to make meaningful contributions, whether in the workplace or through my own ventures.

I love how tech and global perspectives can intersect, and I’m constantly exploring ways to grow both professionally and personally.

When I’m not working on a project or honing my skills you’ll find me exploring new ways to integrate technology with everyday life. Whether it's through freelance projects, diving into data analysis, or collaborating with others, I’m always looking for exciting opportunities to make a real impact.
